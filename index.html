MakAir, Covid-19 Ventilator

Open source, open data & open hardware
The Covid-19 ventilator from the +250 "Makers for Life".

Documentation Upvote on Product Hunt

Product

We designed MakAir, an artificial ventilator, open source, reproducible at low cost, in order to help the intensive care physicians to welcome as many patients as possible.

Project

Why did we create MakAir?

Follow us in our journey to develop the Covid-19 ventilator...

Covid-19 global pandemic

We understood that because of the Coronavirus global pandemic going on,...

Ventilators shortage

...the risk of ventilators shortage was very high.

There were existing solutions,

The respirators on the market pose risks of aerosolization or pulmonary complications.

even open source ones...

Interesting but for short periods.

... and even really innovative ones.

But none was solving the problem from start to finish.

Therefore we came up with an idea!

An open source ventilator, reproducible at low cost, anywhere on Earth.

Complying with regulations and hospitals real-life needs.

Easy to build, easy to use and easy to maintain.

Simplest conception possible.

Based on two modules: the electronic one and the pneumatic one.
